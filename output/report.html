How to Read This Report

This report presents your laboratory results in a clear, visual format. Here is a quick guide to understanding each element.

🎯
Overall Health Score
A number from 0 to 100 that summarises all your test results into one metric. 70–100 is healthy, 40–69 needs monitoring, and below 40 requires prompt attention.
🟢
Severity Badges
STABLE — All parameters within healthy range. MONITOR — One or more parameters are slightly out of range; discuss with your doctor. CRITICAL — One or more parameters require immediate clinical attention.
🌡️
The Speedometer (Gauge)
The dial shows where your result sits across three zones: Red (low / attention), Amber (borderline / monitor), and Green (healthy / normal). The needle points to your score.
📏
Range Sliders
A horizontal bar showing the reference range (normal zone) and where your value falls. A dot marker outside the green zone indicates an abnormal result.
⚠️
Abnormal Parameters
A parameter is flagged as high, low, or critical when it falls outside the reference range for your age and gender. Always discuss abnormal results with a qualified healthcare provider.
📋
Profile Sections
Results are grouped by body system (e.g. Lipid Profile, Thyroid, Blood Sugar). Each section has its own score and severity badge so you can quickly see which system needs attention.
Important: This report is generated by Smart Health Engine and is intended as a summary aid. It does not constitute medical advice. Please consult your physician or specialist for interpretation and treatment decisions.
